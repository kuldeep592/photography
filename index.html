Who I Am

About

Driven by curiosity,
guided by light

I'm Kuldeep Behera, a professional photographer based in India with a deep passion for chasing light across landscapes, cities, and wildlife. My journey began with a borrowed camera and a fascination with the way light transforms the ordinary into the extraordinary.

Every frame I capture tells a story — of fleeting moments, dramatic skies, and the raw beauty that surrounds us. I specialize in landscape, wildlife, portrait, and rural photography, bringing a cinematic eye to every project.

What I Do

Services

Landscape Photography

Immersive wide-angle landscapes capturing the grandeur of mountains, coastlines, and golden-hour vistas with cinematic depth.

Wildlife & Nature

Patient, immersive wildlife sessions capturing animals in their natural habitat — from birds in flight to intimate macro worlds.

Portrait Sessions

Editorial and fine-art portrait sessions with dramatic lighting, bringing out character and emotion in every subject.

Photo Editing & Retouching

Professional color grading, compositing, and retouching to elevate raw captures into gallery-ready masterpieces.

Cinematic Video

Short-form cinematic video work — travel reels, brand stories, and atmospheric b-roll with a photographer's eye for detail.

Workshops & Mentorship

One-on-one mentoring and group workshops covering composition, lighting, post-processing, and developing your creative voice.
